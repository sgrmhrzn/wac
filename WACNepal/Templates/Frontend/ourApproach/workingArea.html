Our Approach
WAC-Nepal strives to uplift the people who are under the poverty and yet not to have the right and opportunity by the government. From this to enhance the capacity and uplift their lives standard .The organization has operating and implementing the various projects regarding to the actual need of the people. In order to fulfill vision, mission and goal of WAC, the following areas of intervention are identified:

Sustainable Livelihood

Health, Drinking Water and Hygiene

Education and Rights

Climate Change and Natural Disaster Management

Capacity Building, Organizational Strengthening and Good Governance
